Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly ("we", "us") collects when you use our personal expense tracking service ("the Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly when you create an account, such as your name and email address, along with the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, such as your browser type and the pages you visit, to keep the Service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve the Service — to save your expenses, generate category breakdowns and monthly summaries, and personalize your experience. We may use your email address to send important account or service-related notices. We do not sell your personal financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which we will remove it except where retention is required by law.

4. Third Party Services

We may rely on trusted third-party providers — for example, hosting or infrastructure services — to operate Spendly. These providers only access your data as needed to perform services on our behalf and are expected to safeguard it. We do not share your personal financial information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will do our best to respond promptly and address your concerns.
